## What the Content Operations benchmark measures

The Content Operations benchmark measures several key aspects of content operations within small service businesses in Austin. It evaluates the efficiency, effectiveness, and quality of content creation, management, and distribution processes.

The scoring model focuses on areas such as content planning, creation, approval, publishing, and performance tracking. It also considers the use of technology, team collaboration, and adherence to best practices.

Understanding what the benchmark measures helps Austin's marketing leads identify areas for improvement and make data-driven decisions to enhance their content operations.

## Signals that move benchmark scores

Several signals significantly impact a business's Content Operations benchmark score. These include but are not limited to:

- **Content Planning**: A well-defined content strategy that aligns with business goals and target audience needs.

- **Content Creation**: Efficient and high-quality content creation processes, utilizing skilled resources and tools effectively.

- **Content Approval**: Streamlined approval processes that minimize delays and maintain content quality.

- **Publishing & Distribution**: Efficient publishing and distribution processes that ensure content reaches the intended audience on time and through the correct channels.

- **Performance Tracking**: Regular tracking and analysis of content performance metrics to inform content strategy and improvement.

## What to improve before the next review

Based on the insights gained from the Content Operations benchmark, Austin's marketing leads should prioritize the following actions before the next review:
